Register with the police

You must register with the police within 7 days of you:

  • arriving in the UK if you applied for a visa from outside the UK
  • being notified of your new status if you were already in the UK when you applied

Even if you have previously registered, you must do so again. You must take your decision notice with you. Visit UK visas and registering with the police for information on where to go, what other documents to take and how to report any change to your circumstances.

If your personal details change

You must tell us about any changes to your personal details, such as your name or nationality. You should also tell us if your sign-in or travel document details change, so that you can continue to access your information online.

You can use manage account to tell us about changes to your:

  • email address
  • phone number
  • home address
  • passport or travel document details, including change of name or nationality
