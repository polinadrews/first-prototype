Before you continue

You've told us you want to register a new identity document to your UK Visas and Immigration account.

To request this update, you'll need to:

  1. Give us the details of your new identity document.
  2. Upload a photo or a scan of your new identity document.

We will need to check the information you give us.

We'll email you once we have added your new identity document to your account, or to let you know if there was a problem.

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "While we check your request, you must continue to use your previous document details to sign in.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

After we have added your document

You must then sign in with the details of the new identity document. The previous details will no longer work.

When you travel, you can now use any of the identity documents registered to your account (as long as they meet the rules on entering the UK). This includes the identity document you applied with and any others you told us about since then.
