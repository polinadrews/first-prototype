Varying your application

If you have submitted an application but now need a different type of permission, you can vary your application instead of cancelling it.

Your current permission will continue while you vary your application.

To vary your application you must:

  • have already submitted an application but not yet received a decision
  • submit a new application for the permission you need and pay the applicable fees

When we receive your new application, we will:

  • send you a confirmation email
  • refund your previous application fee if you have not already provided biometrics
  • refund your previous immigration health surcharge if you paid it
