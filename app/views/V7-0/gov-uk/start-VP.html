View your immigration status

If you have a UK Visas and Immigration account you can use this service to check what rights you have in the UK. For example, you can check if you have the right to live, work, rent, or claim benefits.

Prove your immigration status

Use this service if someone asks you to prove your:

  • right to rent
  • right to work
  • immigration status

You might need to prove your immigration status if you apply for:

  • benefits
  • tax credits
  • a driving licence
  • social housing
  • a place at school, college, or university

You can give them access to the information they need by creating a share code. Email them the share code and your date of birth.

Who can use this service

You can use this service if you have a UK Visas and Immigration account.

You will have a UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) account if you applied:

  • to the EU Settlement Scheme
  • for a visa using the ID Check app to scan your identity document on your phone

Who cannot use this service

You cannot use this service if you can only prove your immigration status with one of these:

  • a vignette in your passport
  • a biometric residence permit or card

If you have a biometric residence permit or card, you can use different services to prove your right to work to an employer and to prove your right to rent to a landlord.

Sign in to your UK Visas and Immigration account

You need to sign in to your account to view and prove your immigration status.

You will need:

  • the identity document number registered to your account
  • access to the mobile number or email address registered to your account
Sign in

Before you start

You will be signed out of this service after 30 minutes of inactivity.

Problems signing in

If you have lost access to your phone number and email address, or have lost your identity document number, contact us to recover your account.
