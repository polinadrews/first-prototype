Your right to work

Right to work

Olivia Lesotho

You can work in the UK until 30 September 2024.

Details

On your current visa, you must work for your sponsor while you are in the UK.

Conditions

You can also work in a part-time job (up to 20 hours per week) if it is one of the following:

  • the same role as your current job
  • on the Shortage Occupation List

Your part-time job must be outside the hours you work for your sponsor.

You can also:

  • do voluntary work – this is unpaid except for reasonable expenses
  • complete the notice period for a job you were doing when you applied

These conditions are the standard requirements for your visa.


! Warning Do not give this page to your employer, it is not proof of your right to work.

To share your details with an employer, you need to create a share code.

If any of your details or other information on this page is not correct, contact the UKVI Resolution Centre.
