Your rights in the UK

You were granted skilled worker status on 1 February 2021. You can work in the UK until 13 January 2024.

Your National Insurance number is AB123456C.

As a skilled worker, you can:

  • work in the job you are sponsored for
  • work in another job for up to 20 hours a week as long as it has got the same occupation code as your main role, or is a job on the Shortage Occupation List.
  • complete the notice period of a contract you had already started before you made your application
  • do voluntary work

You cannot:

  • work as a doctor or dentist in training except on a Health Education England foundation programme
  • work as an entertainer
  • work as a professional sportsperson or coach
  • run a business or be self-employed unless you have applied for a start-up visa
If your name is spelt wrong or your photo is not correct, contact the Immigration help desk.
