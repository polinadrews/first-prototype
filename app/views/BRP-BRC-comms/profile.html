Your right to work

Right to work

Bella-Rose Samantha Wallis

You have permission to work in the UK from 19 October 2021. You can only be employed on a work placement which is part of the course your student visa is based on.

The work placement must be:

  • a compulsory part of your course
  • assessed as part of your course

You may also be able to volunteer or work for a student union.

Prove your right to work

If you need to prove your right to work, you can do this online with a share code.

If any of your details or information on this page is not correct, contact UK Visas and Immigration.
