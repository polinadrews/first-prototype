Changes to right to work checks

From 6 April 2022, you cannot prove your right to work using a physical:

  • biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • biometric residence card (BRC)
  • Frontier Worker permit

This includes a document that shows a later expiry date.

If you have a BRP, BRC or Frontier Worker permit, you must use this service to prove your right to work.
